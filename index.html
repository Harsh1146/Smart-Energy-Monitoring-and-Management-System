// Project

About the Project

Most households lack real-time visibility into energy consumption at the appliance level. Traditional electricity meters only provide total monthly usage, making it nearly impossible to identify energy wastage or inefficient devices.

This project develops a low-cost IoT-based energy monitoring system using the NodeMCU ESP8266 microcontroller paired with a PZEM-004T sensor module. The system measures voltage, current, active power, and cumulative energy consumption in real-time.

Data is transmitted wirelessly to the Blynk IoT cloud platform and visualized on a live mobile and web dashboard. A relay module enables remote switching of appliances. Future scope includes threshold-based automated alerts and multi-room expansion.
