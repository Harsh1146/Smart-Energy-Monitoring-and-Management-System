Project overview
Most households lack real-time visibility of energy consumption at the appliance level. This project builds an IoT-based solution using the NodeMCU ESP8266 microcontroller and PZEM-004T energy sensor to track voltage, current, power, and energy consumption in real time. Data is transmitted over Wi-Fi to the Blynk IoT cloud and displayed on a user-friendly mobile dashboard. A relay module enables basic automation — triggering alerts or switching appliances when power exceeds a threshold.
